I am a Lab Manager for Azimuth Corporation, contracted to Wright Patterson Air Force Base, where I manage the ins and outs of day-to-day lab work. Our research focues on the development of a machine called a Laser Heated Pedestal Growth system (LHPG), and my job revolves around making our scientists' jobs easier. In pursuit of this, I wear tons of different hats from day to day. I develop software solutions that are implemented into our systems that help them conduct research, show off our research, and improve automation functionality of the LHPG over the entirety of the SDLC. Along with this, I've had the opportunity to do mechanical design, research, and implementation from start to finish.
